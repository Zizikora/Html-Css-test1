Apple pie Ingredents
.3/4 cup sugar
.2 tbsp lemon juice
.1 lemon.1/2 tsp cinnamon
.2 tbsp apricot brandy.pinch of salt
.5 baking apples (pared cored thinly sliced)
.2 oz melted butter
.1 unbaked pie shell(9-in diameter)
.1/2 Ib apricot jam
.1 oz suger		 Directions
1.Mix the sugar lemon juice, lemon zest, cinnamon, brandy and salt together
2.Place apples in pie shell neatly,then pour the sugar mixure over the apple
3. Brush with melted butter and bake at 450 degrees for 10 minutes
4. Reduce heat to 350 degrees anf then back for 30 to 40 minutes until golden
5. Combine the jam and suger in sauce panm and then heat to boiling
6.Push the mixture through fine strainer then heat to boiling again and brush over baked pie
Strawberry cupcakes Ingredients
.3 cups all-purpose flour ((360g))
.2 teaspoons baking powder
.1/4 teaspoon baking soda
.1/2 teaspoon salt
.1 cups of sugar
.1 cup butter unsalted (room temperature (227g))
.8 strawberries (sliced and cut)
.whole milk
.2 poached eggs
.strawberry sauce
.whipped cream		 Directions
1. Put the flower in bowl
2. Mix baking powder,baking soda,salt,sugar and butter together in a new bowl
3.Cut strawberries in plate
4. Mix whole milk and eggs together
5.Blend whipped cream until flufy then add strawberry sauce and put strawberry a on top
